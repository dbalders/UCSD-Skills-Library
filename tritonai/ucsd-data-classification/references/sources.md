## UCSD campus references

- **PPM 135-7, Security for Electronic Information at UCSD** —
  https://adminrecords.ucsd.edu/ppm/docs/135-7.pdf
  > ⚠️ Dated 2003 and written against the *old* IS-3 model (Restricted/Unrestricted ×
  > Essential/Required/Deferrable). Its **classification vocabulary is superseded** by the
  > current P1–P4 / A1–A4 system; do not use its grid for classification. Some logical-
  > security *principles* (derived data inherits its source's protection; logs may hold
  > sensitive data; encrypt restricted data in transit and at rest) remain valid and are
  > reflected in this skill.
