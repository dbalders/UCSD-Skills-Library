## Tool Pointers

Use the UCSD Tools page first, then follow its links as needed.

- Campus CMS and Simple Sites for centrally supported accessible website platforms.
- Siteimprove for registered-site scans, site quality issues, and UC-provided training.
- UC San Diego Accessible Brand Color Combinations for branded contrast checks.
- NVDA and VoiceOver for screen-reader testing.

## Source Use Rules

- Prefer UCSD pages for campus scope, roles, timelines, checklists, tools, reporting, and exceptions.
- Use WCAG 2.1 AA for technical success criteria when UCSD guidance points to the standard or the task needs criterion-level detail.
- Do not paste long policy excerpts into the skill output. Summarize and link to the official page.
- If a page has changed, follow the live page rather than this reference file.
